【Introduction】

Where do legends go when they are forgotten? Stories that people no longer tell? They all end up here, in the Spiral.

Further Down the Spiral is a fantasy RPG where players take on the role of wretcheds, personifications of forgotten legends. Here, they must cling to their existence in a world literally spiraling towards oblivion. Will you spread your legend or perish in the attempt?

【Features】

  • Players may create their own custom abilities via the Rhetoric system, allowing them to make truly unique characters. Contains several guidelines and a list of premade Rhetoric.
  • In Conflict, each participant gains a number of dice which they may spend on Actions and Reactions. Turning a quick potshot into a carefully aimed snipe is as simple as adding more dice to your attack.
  • Combat Zones keep combat simple yet tactical and eliminate the need to use a grid.
  • Rules for Stealth and Social Conflict. By using Special Points (SP) you are not instantly discovered for failing a single Stealth Check.
  • All types of Equipment have distinct advantages / disadvantages rather than simple damage calculations. For example, a sword can be used to defend in melee, whereas axes give bonuses to all out attacks.
  • Use the Unknown Spiral Generation rules to create new areas and challenges on the fly.
  • 8 Covenants that grant mechanical and narrative benefits for joining.
