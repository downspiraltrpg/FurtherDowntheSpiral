【Your Character】

【Concept】

Your Concept describes who and what you are, and determines how the Spiral and its denizens perceive you. For example, Charismatic Swashbuckler

【Flaw】

Your Flaw is a personality quirk, ailment, or anything else that gets you into trouble. By invoking your Flaw, you may remove Stress. For example, Tends to ramble

【Essence Dice】

Essence Dice are a pool of five 10-sided dice you use during Conflict to spend on Actions and Reactions each Round. Actions are flexible to give the choice to be aggressive or defensive and can be delayed or split, but any unspent dice are lost at the end of the Round.

【Stress】

Stress represents physical damage, mental fatigue, luck running out, and other consequences. All PCs have 8 Stress Boxes; 5 of which are Light with the remaining 3 Heavy. These are noted as □ and ■ respectively.

Upon taking damage you must check off a number of Stress Boxes equal to the damage where one Light Box is equal to 1 damage and one Heavy Box is equal to 2 damage.

Upon checking off all Stress Boxes, you gain 1 Decay.

【Setbacks】

Setbacks are consequences caused by checking off a Heavy Stress Box. Some examples include broken equipment, an injury that causes you trouble, sealed Rhetoric, or some other complication.

【Attributes】

Attributes represent your wretched’s ability and used whenever you make a Check. There are four Primary Attributes which can be improved by spending XP, and three Secondary Attributes which are increased indirectly

Primary Attributes

  • Body – Strength and Endurance. Used for physical activity such as running, climbing, swimming, attacking with melee weapons, avoiding physical attacks, and Capacity
  • Mind – Intelligence and Perception. Used for determining extent of knowledge, puzzle solving, finding hidden routes, determining number of Skills, and Initiative.
  • Soul – Willpower and Charisma. Used for diplomacy, intimidation, bravery, intuition, and resisting supernatural effects.
  • Teq – Technique and Finesse. Used for artistry, crafting items, attacking with ranged and finesse weapons, lock picking, and stealth.
Secondary Attributes
  • Capacity – Number of significant items you may carry. Your Capacity is equal to Body + 1
  • Wealth – Overall purchasing power. Fluctuates over the course of the campaign based on the treasure and knowledge you find, favors you perform, and other bargaining chips you possess.
  • Decay – Loss of Essence. Upon reaching 4 Decay your wretched is lost to the Great Below.

【Skills】

Skills grant a +1 Bonus on all relevant Checks. For example, if you have the Strength Skill, you get a +1 Bonus towards a Check for lifting a heavy object.

【Rhetoric】

Rhetoric grants the ability to manipulate the rules, narrative, world, NPCs, metalogic, and other esoteric benefits. The lowest Rank is 1 and the highest Rank is 3.

Notes as Name (Rank) ― Description

【Notes】

The Notes section is used for miscellaneous notes, things you wish to remember, and minor items that do not consume Capacity.

【Playing the Game】

【Checks】

Choose an appropriate Attribute and roll any amount of remaining Essence Dice (Checks outside of Conflict always use 5 dice), then compare the results to the Attribute.
Each result less than or equal to the Attribute count as a success.
Results of 1 always succeed and results of 9 and 10 always fail regardless of any Bonuses or Penalties.

Free Checks
Uses only one die but does not consume any Essence Dice. Always pass or fail.

Bonuses and Penalties
Each Bonus increases your Attribute by 1. For example, a +1 Bonus towards a Body(4) Check will succeed on results of 5 or lower.

Each Penalty decreases your Attribute by 1. For example, a -2 Penalty towards a Mind(5) Check will only succeed on results of 3 or under.

【Wealth Checks】

To purchase an item or service, you must make a Check using Wealth as the Attribute. If the number of successes match or succeed the DR of the item/service, you purchase it successfully and suffer no consequence. Otherwise, the transaction has failed.
However, you may turn failed rolls into successes by reducing your Wealth by 1 for each success.

【Invoking Your Flaw】

You may invoke your Flaw by putting yourself into a significant narrative disadvantage. In doing so, you may recover checked Stress Boxes based on how much trouble you are in; the more trouble, the more you recover.
